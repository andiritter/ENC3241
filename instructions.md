# Instructions

### How to Change a Mazda CX-5 Key Fob Battery  

Materials:  
CR2025 or CR2032 3V lithium coin battery  
Small flathead screwdriver  


Steps:  
  
Step 1: Slide the small black knob on the silver underside of the key to open the front black panel of the key fob  

<p align="center">
  <img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/346b4c9a-72fc-47cb-8fd8-840604b0e4f8" />
  <br>
  <sub>Figure 1. Knob to access auxiliary key. (Source: Andrea Ritter) </sub>
</p>
 

Step 2: Remove auxiliary key.  
Step 3: With screwdriver, press down the notch in the bottom right to begin separating the front from the rest of the fob.  

<p align="center">
	<img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/151cd834-02a7-4cc3-adcf-7ef503635808" />
	<br>
	<sub>Figure 2. Using screw driver to press inside notch. (Source: Andrea Ritter)</sub>
</p>  

Step 4: Gently pry open the case to remove the back cover with the silver Mazda emblem. Use fingers or a small screwdriver.  

<p align="center">
	<img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dfbccc96-3d33-4aa8-be88-f5fd1a403fb3" />
	<br>
	<sub>Figure 3. Prying back cover off. (Source: Andrea Ritter)</sub>
</p>  

Step 5: Carefully pry open the circular battery cover with fingers or small screwdriver at the bottom end.  

<p align="center">
  <img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e1699d11-f242-4e03-9d81-532cc2b76fb1" />
  <br>
  <sub>Figure 4. Open battery cover. (Source: Andrea Ritter)</sub>
</p>  

Step 6: Slide out the old battery.  
Step 7: Slide in new battery with positive side facing up towards you (marked with a “+”).  

<p align="center">
  <img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/96da47db-859d-4b5d-a507-f6fbbf301ca9" />
  <br>
  <sub>Figure 5. Insert new battery. (Source: Andrea Ritter) </sub>
</p> 

Step 8: Pop the battery cover back on.  
Step 9: Press the back cover with the Mazda emblem back in place.  
Step 10: Place auxiliary key back inside key fob.  

<p align="center">
  <img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b1d83300-366f-4feb-b150-4c80f1f80b88" />
  <br>
  <sub>Figure 6. Place auxiliary key back. (Source: Andrea Ritter)</sub>
</p>  

Step 11: Slide cover over the auxiliary compartment in direction of arrow (listen for a click).  

<p align="center">
  <img width="200" alt="unnamed" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/af80e706-b1ea-4a6a-80b2-7981bdb71257" />
  <br>
  <sub>Figure 7. Arrow giving direction to slide cover back. (Source: Andrea Ritter)</sub>
</p>
  
	
Step 12: Press lock/unlock buttons to confirm red LED light on fob flashes (indicates battery is correctly changed).
